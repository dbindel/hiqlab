Types can be for input, output, inout, or return.
Everything is okay for input.

Basic types:
 - Objects (return)
 - Numeric arrays (inout, output, return)
 - Numeric scalars (return)
 - Strings (inout, output, return)
 - mxArrays (return, output)

Pointer types:
 - To objects (return)
 - To numeric scalars (inout, output, return)

Reference types:
 - To objects (return)
 - To numeric scalars (inout, output, return)
